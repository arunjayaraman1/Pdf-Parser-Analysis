#### **Important Note:**

- There are total 10 calendar holidays for the year 2026, equally divided into fixed and optional holidays.
- Team Members are requested honour the Fixed Public holidays.
- Team Members can avail any 5-optional leaves from the list of Optional Public Holiday in the calendar Year 2026.
- Supervisor/ Team leads can use their discretion on the Holidays as per the business exigencies and personal requirement of the TeamMember.
- All the optional holidays can be availed only after the approval is received by the manager on KEKA portal.
- Team members are requested to plan leaves and holidays ahead of time so as to avoid situations where leave needs to be compulsorily taken at the end of the year.
